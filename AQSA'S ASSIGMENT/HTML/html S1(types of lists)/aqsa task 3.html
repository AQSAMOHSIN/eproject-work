Simple Sponge Cake

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 100g caster suger
  • 85g self-raising flour

Notes on indegredients

Castor sugar
Fine;y granulated white sugar
Self-raising flour
A pre-mixed combination of flour and leavening agent(usually salt and baking powder).

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 190C.
  2. Grease a 20cm round cake pan.
  3. In a medium bowl,whip together the eggs nd castor sugar until the fluffy.
  4. fold in flour.
  5. Pour mixture into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes in the preheated oven ,or until the top of the cake springs back when lightly pressed.
  7. Cool in the pan over a wire rack.
